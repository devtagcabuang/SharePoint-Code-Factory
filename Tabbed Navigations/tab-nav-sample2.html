Style It!

Tabbed Navigation can also be done in other ways. In this sample, we used the "Folder" style Tabbed Navigation.

Although Tab Nav is a convenient way to add materials, we recommend to avoid placing wordy content. If your content contains too many words, just simply add a button that redirects to a page as a continuation of your content that contains the full details of your topic.

PROS: Aesthetic Appeal

Tabs often contribute to a visually appealing interface. They can be customized to match the overall design and branding of the application, enhancing the user experience and making the interface more engaging.

PROS: Flexibility & Scalability

Tab Nav is flexible and scalable, accommodating various types and amounts of content.

Additional tabs can be added or removed as needed, making it adaptable to changing requirements and expanding functionality.

But also keep in mind that we need to follow Merck's Branding Guidelines!

PROS: Direct Access to Information

Each tab represents a specific area of content or functionality. Users can directly access the desired information by selecting the corresponding tab, eliminating the need for sequential browsing or searching.
