Why Tabbed Navigation?

Tabbed Navigation is a good way to divide large amounts of information into manageable chunks. It's also a great way to present topics that can be divided into several sub-topics.

It is also possible to include images or logos that correspond to the topic of your tabs.

Progressive Disclosure

Tabs enable progressive disclosure of information, presenting the most essential or commonly accessed content int the primary tabs while providing less frequently used options in secondary or nested tabs.

This helps maintain a clean and focused interface.

Clear Organization

Tabs provide a visual and conceptual organization of content or functionalities. Users can easily understand the different sections or categories available and navigate between them.

Compact & Space-efficient

Tabs allow you to present multiple options within a limited space. They help conserve screen real estate and are particular useful in responsive designs for smaller devices.
